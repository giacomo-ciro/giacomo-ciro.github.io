Medical Image Annotation Tool

This tool allows you to annotate regions of interest in medical images to help Jack and Dave in their computer vision project!

As an expert, your task is to annotate the regions of the image which help you determine whether or not a pathology is present/

Instructions: 1. Click and drag to draw on the image 2. Release to complete a shape 3. Click the "Submit Annotation" button when finished 4. Send the saved images to giacomociro02@gmail.com as they are 5. Thank you!
